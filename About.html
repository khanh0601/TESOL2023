ABOUT VUS

From its foundation in 1997, VUS has grown to become the leading English language training institution in Vietnam and now aims to become the leading branded English language and tutoring provider in the country.

VUS’s mission is to provide high-quality English language and tutoring education to children, teenagers, and adults at accessible pricing through proven techniques that are certiﬁed and internationally recognized in a superior and engaging learning environment.

VUS is proud to become a global standard English language training unit recognized by NEAS, an independent organization that manages the quality of international English teaching centers.
